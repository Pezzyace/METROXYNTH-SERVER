How to buy

Step 1: Install Wallet

Confirm you have a MetaMask wallet installed on your browser, or use a wallet supported by Wallet Connect (Trust Wallet recommended). If you do not have a MetaMask wallet you can install one here.

We suggest using MetaMask when making your purchase on desktop for a smoother experience.

Trust Wallet is ideal for buying on mobile. We recommend connecting through Trust Wallet's built in browser.

Step 2: Connect Wallet

When your wallet is ready, click the “Connect Wallet” button on the website and select the appropriate option. If you are using a mobile wallet app you will need to select “Wallet Connect”.

Step 3: Buy with Solana

When you have the SOL in your wallet you can now swap it for METROXYNTH tokens. Type in the amount of METROXYNTH you want to buy ($10 minimum) and then click “Buy with SOL”. Your wallet provider will ask you to confirm the transaction and show the cost of the gas fee to complete the transaction.

Step 4: Claim your Tokens

Once the Presale has ended, To claim your tokens simply click on the claim button after you have connected your wallet to the site.
